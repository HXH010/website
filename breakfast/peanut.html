Peanut Butter & Chia Berry Jam Muffin

Indulge in the perfect balance of savory and sweet with this hearty yet delicious English muffin. Toasted to golden perfection, the muffin is generously spread with creamy, rich peanut butter, offering a satisfying, nutty flavor. Topping it all off is a luscious layer of chia berry jam, made with ripe, juicy berries and nutritious chia seeds, creating a delightful blend of sweetness and texture. Whether enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack, this combination of creamy, fruity, and crunchy elements makes for a wholesome treat that's as satisfying as it is flavorful. A heartwarming bite of comfort with every mouthful!
